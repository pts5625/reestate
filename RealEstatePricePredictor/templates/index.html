How It Works

This tool uses machine learning to predict apartment prices in Dhaka and Gazipur city based on:

  • Location: The area where the property is situated
  • Floor Area: The size of the property in square feet
  • Bedrooms: The number of bedrooms in the property
  • Bathrooms: The number of bathrooms in the property
  • Floor Number: The floor on which the property is located

Our model was trained on real estate data from Dhaka and Gazipur to provide accurate price estimates in lakhs (1 lakh = 100,000).
